# Design Requirements

**Type:** Authoritative visual-and-lore design spec.
**Scope:** What the world, characters, and their core visual attributes must communicate. This document defines canon-facing design requirements, not rendering implementation or prompt wording.

**Source of truth inputs:** [`data/channel/generated/mushroom-lore.md`](../data/channel/generated/mushroom-lore.md), [`data/channel/generated/general-lore-context.md`](../data/channel/generated/general-lore-context.md), [`data/channel/characters/*/manifest.json`](../data/channel/characters), and [`data/channel/generated/character-art-pack/analysis.md`](../data/channel/generated/character-art-pack/analysis.md).

Last verified against source materials: 2026-04-13.

---

## 1. World Identity

- **1-A.** The world is **Mycelium**: a fungal-biological setting where biology has replaced conventional geology.
- **1-B.** The world's central landmark is **YGG-MYCEL / Игг-Мицель**, a colossal black tree that functions as both world-organism and command center.
- **1-C.** The atmosphere must read as **spore-saturated and bioluminescent**, with recurring violet, green, amber, bone, and fungal-neutral tones.
- **1-D.** The setting must feel **organic, symbiotic, and slightly entropic**, not industrial, medieval-generic, or sci-fi-clean.
- **1-E.** Worldbuilding should consistently reinforce the core cycle of **growth, decay, transformation, and reuse**.

---

## 2. Shared Mushroom-Elf Character Rules

- **2-A.** The five main heroines are all **mushroom-elves**, not humans with mushroom accessories.
- **2-B.** If ears are visible in an image, they must be **elf ears**.
- **2-C.** Mushroom traits must be integrated into the body, hair, clothing, magic, or silhouette as **biological identity markers**, not added as disconnected props.
- **2-D.** Each heroine must remain readable first as a **distinct individual**, and second as part of one shared fungal civilization.
- **2-E.** Character designs may vary in skin tone, palette, and styling, but they must stay within the world's fungal/organic visual language.
- **2-F.** Eyes, spores, fungal growths, mycelium, decay effects, or symbiotic materials should read as **alive** and world-native rather than cosmetic fantasy decoration.

---

## 3. Shared Visual Language

- **3-A.** The overall design direction is **dark-fairytale fungal fantasy**, not generic high fantasy and not hard sci-fi.
- **3-B.** Every main character must have a **strong silhouette** that is readable without relying on text labels.
- **3-C.** Every main character must have at least one **signature visual motif** tied to her role:
  - Thalla: gold mycelium / biostasis / sacred fungal regalia
  - Lomië: portals / star-spores / lattice mushrooms
  - Kirt: toxic archery / aggressive horned silhouette / neon fungal accents
  - Dalamar: porcelain decay / ash / severe entropy iconography
  - Axylin: fermentation / alchemy / ginger-chaos asymmetry
- **3-D.** Mushroom-world materials should recur across designs: mycelium threads, spores, fungal caps, bone-like growth, bark, resin, rot, glassy organic structures, and living textiles.
- **3-E.** Costumes and props must support the character's function in the circle; they must not feel fashion-random or lore-detached.
- **3-F.** Group presentation should read as one **fungal circle / system** with complementary roles rather than five unrelated designs.

---

## 4. Social and Lore Structure

- **4-A.** The five heroines collectively function as a **single interdependent circle** centered on YGG-MYCEL.
- **4-B.** Each heroine must have a defined domain within the world structure:
  - Thalla: trunk / heart / throne / golden garden
  - Lomië: upper void / pathmaking / crystalline cocoon
  - Kirt: canopy / watchpost / hunting platform
  - Axylin: amber growth / fermentation grotto / alchemy station
  - Dalamar: roots / crypt / ash-processing cathedral
- **4-C.** Their relationships should read as **allied but tension-bearing**, with role contrast and strong personality friction.
- **4-D.** Visual design should support lore function: environment, tools, posture, and ornamentation should reveal what each heroine does in the system.

---

## 5. Thalla Requirements

- **5-A.** Thalla is the **central sovereign / biostasis architect** of the setting.
- **5-B.** She must read as an **ancient fungal force inhabiting or overtaking an elven body**.
- **5-C.** Her palette must prominently include **gold, white, bone, warm brown, and fungal light**.
- **5-D.** Her face and body should retain the canon markers: **black eyes with fiery-gold life in the iris, gold tear-like spore traces, and luminous gold mycelium across skin**.
- **5-E.** Her design must communicate **regal authority, sacred danger, and organic assimilation**, not brute violence or chaotic mess.
- **5-F.** Her associated spaces and props must support her role as ruler, cultivator, and assimilator: **Golden Reliquary, living garden, spore-laden regalia, symbiotic armor, relics, and trophied remains**.

---

## 6. Lomië Requirements

- **6-A.** Lomië is the **guide / architect of paths and dimensional routes**.
- **6-B.** Her design must communicate **distance, precision, and spectral elegance** rather than raw force.
- **6-C.** Her palette must center on **deep green, indigo, violet, silver, white spores, and cold portal light**.
- **6-D.** Her silhouette and effects must support **space-cutting and portal magic**, especially through flowing layers, drifting spores, lattice mushroom forms, and void-like accents.
- **6-E.** Her signature artifact is the **Triple Knot of the Abyss** staff with lattice-mushroom forms containing void energy.
- **6-F.** Her environment must read as elevated, crystalline, and threshold-like: **glass cocoon, black reflective floor, portal points, floating spores**.

---

## 7. Kirt Requirements

- **7-A.** Kirt is the **aggressive huntress / strike force** of the circle.
- **7-B.** Her design must read as **toxic punk bio-archery**, not noble ranger classicism.
- **7-C.** Her palette must strongly feature **black, violet, neon green, and venomous fungal contrast**.
- **7-D.** Her silhouette must preserve the canon identity markers: **high aggressive hair, Ramaria-style antler growths, sharp cropped combatwear, and bow-led combat posture**.
- **7-E.** Her weapons and effects must reinforce **predatory fungal ballistics**: spore-burst arrows, crystalline infection, parasitic or toxic follow-through.
- **7-F.** Her environment must read as a high, dangerous defense platform with **traps, trophies, target walls, and lookout energy**.

---

## 8. Dalamar Requirements

- **8-A.** Dalamar is the **entropy emissary / digestive force of the system**.
- **8-B.** Her design must communicate **silence, reduction, ash, and immaculate controlled decay**.
- **8-C.** Her palette must remain anchored in **white, black, gray ash, muted mold-green, and corpse-porcelain contrast**.
- **8-D.** Her face must preserve the canon porcelain markers: **white skin, black sclera, green-yellow fungal sparks, erased white lips, and severe symmetrical eye treatment**.
- **8-E.** Her silhouette should feel **narrow, severe, still, and ceremonial**, with visual restraint stronger than the other heroines.
- **8-F.** Her signature props and spaces must reinforce entropy ritual: **entropy scepter, ash censer, silent crypt, obsidian throne, dust archive**.

---

## 9. Axylin Requirements

- **9-A.** Axylin is the **fermentation alchemist / energetic engine** of the group.
- **9-B.** Her design must read as **volatile, clever, warm, and asymmetrical**, not orderly or courtly.
- **9-C.** Her palette must prioritize **amber, ginger, caramel, honey-orange, glass highlights, and warm fermentation glow**.
- **9-D.** Her face and styling must preserve the canon markers: **cream-toned skin, black sclera, honey-orange eyes, tousled red hair, and experiment-worn makeup**.
- **9-E.** Her tools and accessories must emphasize **alchemy in a fungal world**: bubbling retorts, fermentation bombs, living ingredients, improvised belts, and stained workwear.
- **9-F.** Her design package includes **Ginger**, the living ginger-root companion, as a recurring support motif when the scene allows.
- **9-G.** Her environment must read as warm, pressurized, aromatic, and biologically active: **amber grotto, elastic entry membrane, vessels, brews, and fermenting materials**.

---

## 10. Group Consistency Rules

- **10-A.** The cast must feel like **one coherent set** when shown together, with coordinated world logic and non-overlapping roles.
- **10-B.** Shared motifs across the cast should include fungal biology, black-tree world logic, spores, symbiosis, and organic magic.
- **10-C.** Individual differentiation should come from **role, silhouette, material palette, and magical function**, not from abandoning the shared mushroom-elf identity.
- **10-D.** Any new depiction, prompt, render, UI portrait, or dossier image that contradicts these design rules is a **canon bug** unless the requirements doc is updated in the same change.
